# 📣 We're now on GitHub Sponsors!

We've been building Hummingbird to be the best Swift server framework we can: fast, lightweight, and designed for modern Swift concurrency.

If Hummingbird has been useful to you or your team, sponsoring is one of the most direct ways to help us keep maintaining and improving it. You can find our profile at https://github.com/sponsors/hummingbird-project. There are some small rewards for supporting us. Although the main reward should be you know that you will be supporting us in continuing development of the framework and its related libraries. 

Every contribution, big or small, will help. Thank you for your support. ❤️
